You are analyzing teacher messages in an educational dialogue session.

Your task: Identify which messages are greetings or non-educational
content that should be excluded from pedagogical analysis.

## Greeting Messages (exclude from analysis)

These types of messages should be marked as greetings:
- Opening greetings: "안녕하세요", "Hello", "Hi", "안녕"
- Session openers: "오늘 수업 시작할게요", "Let's begin", "시작하겠습니다"
- Simple acknowledgments without educational content: "네", "응", "OK"
- Farewells: "수고했어요", "잘했어요", "다음에 또 봐요", "Goodbye"
- Pure pleasantries: "어떻게 지내?", "How are you?"

## Pedagogical Messages (include in analysis)

These types of messages should NOT be marked as greetings:
- Questions about concepts or understanding
- Explanations or clarifications
- Probing student thinking
- Feedback on student responses
- Scaffolding questions
- Any message with educational intent
